“Everyone needs computer programming. It will be the way we speak to the servants.”

John McCarthy, the computer scientist who invented the Lisp language and coined the term "Artificial Intelligence"

People have been managing other people for thousands of years


  • The British built their empire by winning the colonies.
  • America has become the economic strength thanks to the immigrant labor cheap during the industrial revolution.
Now we get work done by managing machine

The nature of work has fundamentally changed. human replaced by machine.

  • humans make 3.5 billion Google searches
  • Trip Advisor helps you decide where to go for vacation
  • Expedia helps you book the right flight to get there
  • Google Maps directs you to the airport


It's machine that carry out that work - not man

But machines are only able to do all this work because men tell them exactly what to do.

They need us humans to give them extremely clear instructions.


instruction 1 : do that
instruction 2 : do this
instruction 3 : if condition then action else action_2

That means coding

Coding : the new literacy

It’s the essential 21st century skill that every ambitious person needs to learn if they want to succeed.
